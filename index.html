About Vital Nutrition

Pioneering the circular economy through innovative organic waste valorization

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Vital Nutrition is a dynamic Algerian limited liability company, certified as a startup, specializing in the valorization of organic waste, particularly algae, declassified dates, and olive pomace.

We base our growth on a sustainable industrial model that places the circular economy at the heart of our actions, transforming organic waste into innovative solutions adapted to all types of crops, enabling our farmers to increase their profitability while preserving the environment and human health.

Our approach fits within the framework of national policy for local product substitution to imports, contributing to economic growth while promoting environmental sustainability.

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for sustainable agriculture

Organic Waste Treatment & Valorization

We specialize in transforming organic waste including algae, dates, coffee grounds, and plant debris into valuable agricultural inputs through advanced processing techniques.

Natural Bio-stimulants & Organic Amendments

Development of natural bio-stimulants and organic amendments including compost that enhance soil fertility and plant growth using sustainable methods.

Innovative Biocides Development

Research and development of innovative biocides based on local mycorrhizal strains for effective and environmentally friendly crop protection.

Deficiency Correctors Production

Manufacturing of specialized correctors for nutrient deficiencies including Calcium, Magnesium, Iron, and other essential minerals for optimal plant health.

Agricultural Extension & Support

Providing comprehensive extension services and farmer support to ensure proper application and maximum benefit from our organic solutions.

Quality Control & Analysis

Advanced physico-chemical analysis and quality control services through our MMR Laboratory to ensure product excellence and efficacy.
